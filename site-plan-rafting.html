Overview

Purpose

When you talk about the best destination to do whitewater rafting everyone would mention first places like Wyoming, Idaho, Oregon, or Alaska, but I believe Utah has top destinations to practice this sport. Our purpose is to spread the word and show everyone around the world why Utah should be their top destimnatin to do raftng. The purpose for this website is to share information about the different places where you can practice rafting, and get the attention of tourits.

Audience

My target audience would be people with an age range between 18-40 which are probably the ages of the people who practice rafting the most. We want to target families and individuals that are whiling to spend money in a high level experience. The website will provide easy access to finding a booking the best experience based on their budget and interests. We wont try to target an specific financial target since our purpose is to provide access to any kind of budgets to come to Utah and do rafting. Our main focus will be to develope a functional website that our users can acess from a computer or a mobile device.
